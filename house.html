Uses of smart technology at home:
Some benefits of smart technology at home:

  • It givis you more control over your energy usage and at the same time adjusting the temperature, turning lights on and off, opening and closing shades, adjusting irrigation based on the weather and many others.

  • Smart home assistants: Amazon Echo, Google Home and HomePod are some smart speaker that can control smart homes, answer questions and play musics/videos.
